Dance Project - Part 2

Lead coder & Organizer

For mobile and desktop

This project was the 2nd part of our dance theme, in this project we recieved another groups website, had to work upon it and make it responsive for desktop. This projects theme was a bollywood dancing school.

My main role for this project was organizing our work, being responsible for some of our design choices and helping everyone with coding.

As we recieved the project from the other group, we realised that we had to rework their current design to something new, so we quickly did some brainstorming and got some individual moodboards ready and made our final moodboard.

We then started making sketches and wireframes for our website, made some final mockups with our chosen design and name on it and then we started working on the code. As we choose to redo the other groups work from scratch, it meant that this was the first time we had to make a site for both mobile and desktop.

So we got to work on the mobile version making pages that had an "okay" flow, because we hadnt been totally clear on when to use certain elements, colors and fonts.

The page I was responsible for making, was our sign up page and our menu. As we continued making our website and started to code the desktop version, we slowly finished the site, but as a result of our miscommunication had some flaws in its cohesiveness.
