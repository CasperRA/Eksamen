Rhythm Studio

Dance Project

For mobile and desktop

In this project, we had to make a website for a bollywood dance studio, this was a bit different from previous projects as we received a website from another group, which we then had to improve upon.

The website we received wasn't up to par with our vision for what a bollywood website should look like, so we ended up making a new one from scratch to have a clear design and consistency over our several pages.

After doing research on the bollywood dance we started looking into creating a moodboard as a sort of "design guide" for everyone on the team to follow.

We started coding our website, mobile first, with these design decisions in mind. After being done with the mobile version we made breakpoints to further develop the website for dekstop users. As usual we had a final showcase to show of our work.
