Dance Project - Part 2

Research & Design Managing

For mobile only

As this project was our second project ever, it was all about learning how to make a website for mobile. Our project theme this time, was about a swingdance dance studio and was the first part of our dance themed projects, where in the next part we swap projects and work upon and redo some parts of each others projects.

My main role in this project was doing some primary research, find out what kind of people dance swing and making design decisions.

We started off our research by doing some desk research and ended up finding a little dance school in Copenhagen named "Happy Feet", which one of my project members and I visited. We took inspiration from the vibe at the dance school and from other similar websites to decide what kind of style we were looking and made a color palette.

Then it was time for some sketching, wireframing and mockups, trying to appeal to a vintage crowd while stile being an easy to use modern website.

When we started to code our website we ran into alot of problems, this being our second project and our first project for mobile, it was alot harder to cope with all the new things we had learnt about. But we managed to pull through and got close to what we originally were looking for in our website. It wasn't fully what we wanted out of it though and could definitely be improved upon.

I was responsible for making the page where you are able to see what classes are held when and what the upcomming events are. (The page in the middle, down below)

At the end of this project we had a final showcase and then afterwards we swapped our swingdance website with another groups project, see more about part 2 in the Rhythm Studio project.
