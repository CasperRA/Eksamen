Xenos

Car Project

For mobile and desktop

Xenos was created in 2 parts, first a mobile version based on a target group which we had to pick ourselves. After picking we then designed and created the website for mobile at first and had a showcase to recieve feedback from our work.

After the showcase from part 1, we had a second part, which involved a reevaluating of the good and bad, based on feedback we got.
Part 2 also was about making the website more responsive which also meant we had to make it for desktop as well.

To finish of the project we had a final showcase showing our new content for the website and the changes made in the 2nd part.
