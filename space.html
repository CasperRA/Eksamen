Outer Travels

Space Project

Project Leader and Designer

For desktop only

As our first project, Outer Travels was a space themed project, where we had to make a website around a company, that had some product in space.

My main role in this project, was being the project leader and with us only being 2 out of the 5 people we were supposed to be in this project, we didn't have many opportunities to do something grand.

This project was for desktop only, as an introduction to html coding. We started the project of by brainstorming ways to make a website centered around space, we decided to make a dark color palette and try and draw in a lot of black. We came up with the idea to make a website where you could choose to travel to planets like a vacation.

We then had to make a banner and to not make the website totally black we decided it needed some color. Being a very small team of 2 we decided to start of by making our pages separately. We later learnt that it wasn't ideal to make things separately, as it caused some issues in terms of not having the same vision for the project, when making the website.

I was responsible for creating our landing page with a banner, differnet planets you could travel to and I made a logo to accompany our site to further sell it as a company. One of the major problems in this project was that we had no experience in coding html in groups and didn't have any tools to use, to make it coding together easier for us.

But in the end, we ended up with a project we weren't fully happy with, but we learnt a lot from our mistakes made in this project.
